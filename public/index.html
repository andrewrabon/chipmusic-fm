ChipMusic.fm is an open-source music player for all the amazing Creative Commons chiptunes found on ChipMusic.org. This project is not endorsed by ChipMusic.org and is a passion project by me, Andrew Rabon.

Unlike most music players, ChipMusic.fm is shuffle-only. My hope is that this site will make it easier to discover and share great new tracks from the nearly 8,000 songs that have been uploaded to ChipMusic.org over the years.
